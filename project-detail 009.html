C9 Entertainment’s upcoming boy group EPEX has officially announced their debut date!

May 7, 2021

Are you excited for EPEX’s debut?

EPEX has officially announced that they will be making their debut at 6pm KST on June 8, 2021 with their 1st EP Album named “BIPOLAR Pt.1: Prelude of Anxiety” and their debut show will be aired an hour later on Mnet at 7pm KST on the same day.

Introduction to EPEX

EPEX (이펙스) is an eight-member boy group under C9 Entertainment, which is a home to CIX and Cignature. Yet, Cignature is managed by C9 Entertainment's new music label, J9 Entertainment. EPEX includes former “Produce X 101” contestant, Keum Dong Hyun, who went on to debut as an actor in the web drama “Best Mistake 2” in 2020. The group consists of: Wish, Keum, MU, A-Min, Baekseung, Ayden, Yewang and Jeff.
