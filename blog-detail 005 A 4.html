{Episode 2}
Answer: Not an exceptional unfortunate one

Sisu’s phone suddenly rings. It is from a manager of a store where she is currently working part-time, outside of campus.

[Manager] “Hey Sisu! Can you take both evening and night shifts today? I’ve already tried to contact Minchan, but he didn’t pick my phone. I know you took a day off, but I have an urgent matter. Please..”

[Sisu] “Hmmmm..”

[Manager] “How about you work today and I’ll give you a day off tomorrow and a half-day off for the day after tomorrow. Deal?”

[Sisu] “Okay, then. I’ll be there in 15 minutes.”

………………

She goes back to her part-time job even though she doesn’t feel like working today. As soon as she arrives at the café, it starts raining heavily.

Even though she is working the whole evening, she can’t help thinking about a question that one of her friends asked her a year ago.

[Sisu’s friend] “In this generation where a million people are unemployed, do you still think that you can get a job even after you graduated???”

All the time, Sisu always works hard because she believes that “Hard work will never betray you”, but “so what now? All I am good at is getting a good score, but it won’t work anymore.”

She sighs again and continues working. She worked for 5 hours straight and looks at the clock. It’s 9 pm, so she closed the shop after cleaning everything and goes back home.

As she reached in front of her apartment, she realized that she had forgotten the room key and puts it on the table in front of the class while she is double-checking her CV before going to the interview.

[Sisu] “Damn, I’ve had a long day. My stupid brain forgot to put everything back into my bag..and even the weather is not on my side. I’m tired of everything.”

Even though it is still raining outside, she decided to go back to her faculty building.

The world was complete dark. The black sky with shiny little stars has completely covered with rain clouds. The campus was ghost-quiet, except for the sound of raindrops and the wind lashing at the windows.

Off in the distance, there is a soft rumble of thunder, but that didn’t stop her to go alone as she doesn’t get scared easily.

She goes inside the building. The lights flicker on and off and on again. She keeps trying to press the button of the elevator, but the elevator seems to be broken, so she takes the stairs.

She found her key on the table. She carefully puts it in her bags this time.

At the time, she heard some footsteps coming from the stair. She immediately goes there to see who it was. There was no sign of anyone coming up, but the sound comes from upstairs.
