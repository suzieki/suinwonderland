Gaho comeback with a new single "Rush Hour"

May 16, 2021

Gaho's Comeback with "Rush Hour" on the May 24th 2021

According to Gaho’s agency Planetarium Records, his new song "Rush Hour" will be released at 6 p.m. KST on the May 24, 2021.

  • Since last year, Gaho started to gain popularity with his popular OST song “Start Over” from JTBC drama “Itaewon Class”. He not only topped on the music charts but also won several music awards with “Start Over”.
  • Gaho won K-Wave OST Award with his popular OST song 시작(Start Over) from [ITAEWON CLASS Korean Drama OST Part.2] at 4th Soribada Best K-Music Awards 2020. Again, he also won BEST OST award with Start Over at 22nd Mnet Asian Music Awards (MAMA Awards).
    • As TMI, Gaho birth name is Kang Dae-ho (강대호) and he was born on September 14, 1997. Actually, Gaho and I were born in the same year, but he was born a month earlier than me. He is one of the talented artists with a strong vocal. Please support him a lot.
