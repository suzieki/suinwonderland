Are you a Koreaboo?

What is Koreaboo? How do you if you are one?

April 28, 2021

Koreaboo is similar to ‘Weeaboo’, which is a term used to describe a person who completely disregards their nationality and claims themselves as Japanese.

According to Urban Dictionary, Koreaboo is defined as a non-Koreans “who is obsessed with Korean culture so much. [They] denounce their own culture and call themselves [as] Korean. However, there is a line; if someone just likes the music, language and culture they are not classed as a Koreaboo.” (j.wang 21, 2017). Koreaboo has a negative connotation and people misuse it as an insult not only to Koreaboo but also to those who love K-pop and are learning Korean culture and language with a purpose.

So how do we know if you are not a Koreaboo???


#1 You are not a Koreaboo if you can understand and read both “Annyeonghaseyo” and “안녕하세요”

Koreaboo usually uses Korean words even among people who do not understand Korean words. They learn some Korean words like “Annyeonghaseyo” from watching K-Dramas and from listening to their idols speaking, but they cannot read or write in Korean.


#2 You are not a Koreaboo if you are learning the Korean Language with a purpose

If you have a purpose of studying the Korean language, you should not let people call you Koreaboo. For example, you are learning Korean to study in South Korea or to communicate with your idols or to watch K-Dramas without subtitles or to understand K-pop. If you’re learning Korean for any of those reasons or more, that doesn’t make you a Koreaboo.


#3 Will you become a Koreaboo by just listening to K-pop or watching K-drama?

If someone calls you Koreaboo for loving K-pop and K-drama, just ask them why people don’t call Englishboo or something to people who watch English movies or if they are a fan of Taylor Swift. If they don’t stop teasing you, just consider them as someone who is too dumb to understand your explanation.


#4 You are not a Koreaboo if you don’t try to make yourself look like a Korean or a Korean idol

There are Koreaboo out there who undergo cosmetic surgery to change their appearance so that they will look like a Korean. For example, getting monolids surgery so that they might look like a Korean.





JUST KEEP IT IN MIND THAT THERE ARE FACTORS INVOLVES IN CULTURAL ASSIMILATION AND INTEGRATION IN MIGRANT COMMUNITY IN SOUTH KOREA. SO, YOU SHOULDN’T USE THE KOREABOO EASILY TO EVERYONE.
