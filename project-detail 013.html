C9 Entertainment’s boy group EPEX has officially announced their comeback date!

September 26, 2021

Are you excited for EPEX’s first comeback?

On September 26, EPEX has begun revealing their colourful and bright group concept photo for their first-ever comeback with EPEX's 2nd EP album 'Bipolar Pt.2 - Prelude of Love', which is set to release on October 26 at 6 PM KST. This album consists of four tracks: ‘Love Virus’, ‘Do 4 Me’, ‘Breathtaking’ and ‘Traveller’. The title song is 'Do 4 Me'. It is a punk-pop song with an ear-catching rhythm. The song expresses the complex emotions and bold confession of a boy who fell in love for the first time.

Introduction to EPEX

EPEX (이펙스) is an eight-member boy group under C9 Entertainment, which is a home to CIX and Cignature. Yet, Cignature is managed by C9 Entertainment's new music label, J9 Entertainment. EPEX includes former “Produce X 101” contestant, Keum Dong Hyun, who went on to debut as an actor in the web drama “Best Mistake 2” in 2020. The group consists of: Wish, Keum, MU, A-Min, Baekseung, Ayden, Yewang and Jeff. The group debuted on June 8, 2021, with their first EP titled ‘Bipolar Pt.1: Prelude of Anxiety’ and the title track is ‘Lockdown’.
