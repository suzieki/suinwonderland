BTS’s “Dynamite” Becomes Fastest Korean Group Music Video To Reach 1 Billion Views

April 12, 2021

BTS' 'Dynamite' hits 1 Billion views on YouTube

Congratulations BTS!

BTS is now the very first fastest Korean boy band whose MV hits a billion views on YouTube. The MV was dropped online on August 21, 2020 and hit a billion views on April 12, 2021. With 'Dynamite', BTS not only became the first Kpop band from South Korea to top the Billboard Hot 100 chart but also got Grammy nomination.

If you haven't listened to 'Dynamite', check out the MV on YouTube linked below.

I fell in love with that song on the day it was released. During these hard times, listening to this song could make me forget everything. The song is upbeat, funky and groovy. You can dance to it in a party, at your house wearing pajamas, or on a road trip. Make sure listening to this song once even if you are not an ARMY. Recommended.
