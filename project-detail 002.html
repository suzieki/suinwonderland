IU is back with her fifth studio album LILAC

April 11, 2021

IU Sweeps the Charts with with her 5th studio album LILAC

The album consists a total of 10 songs including the pre-release track “Celebrity“, as well as the title song “Lilac“, Flu“, “Coin“, “Spring Bye Spring“, “Celebrity“, “Turning Song” featuring DEAN, “Empty Cup“, “Child and My Ocean“, “Ah Puh“, and “Epilogue“.

  • On March 9, IU unveiled the track list for her upcoming fifth full album ‘LILAC’ on her official twitter account that was released on March 25 at 6PM KST!
  • The pre-release track "Celebrity" was released on January 27, 2021 through EDAM Entertainment via Kakao M. With just pre-release track song, IU took 12 trophies on music shows so far.
  • Again, IU won four more trophies with the title song 'LILAC' so far. (April 11, 2021)

    • If you haven't listened to IU's songs, check out the MV on YouTube linked below.

I am one of the UAENAs (IU's fandom name). IU has captivated the hearts of me, other UAENAs, colleagues and peers with her comforting music, immense talent and adorable personality. In her more than 12 years in this entertainment industry (IU debut date is September 18, 2008), IU has proven to be a top class artist.

I love all of her songs. In this album, I love her pre-release track 'Celebrity' more than other songs. The lyrics are deep and meaningful.

My favourite one is "잊지마 넌 흐린 어둠 사이 왼손으로 그린 별 하나 보이니 그 유일함이 얼마나 아름다운지 말야 You are my celebrity".
Translated lyrics: "Don't forget among the cloudy dark. You're a star painted with a left hand. Can't you see how beautiful. A true uniqueness can be You are my celebrity"
