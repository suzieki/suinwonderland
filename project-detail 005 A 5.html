Reasons Why You Shouldn’t Date A K-Pop Fan If You……

April 28, 2021

Disclaimer: These are just my opinions, so it might be wrong for some. Don’t take it seriously.


#1 You Shouldn’t Date A K-Pop Fan If You Can’t Handle Your Jealousy.

Most of the Kpop idols are rich in visual and talent. If you have an inferiority complex, it will affect your relationship when you keep comparing yourself with them. It might not be good for you.


#2 You Shouldn’t Date A K-Pop Fan If You Don’t Understand Why Your Partner Like K-Pop

Understanding between lover is important. You need to understand that liking K-pop is just similar to what you love to do like playing games or watching Netflix as entertainment. If you can’t understand or respect what another person likes, it might not be good for your relationship.


#3 You Shouldn’t Date A K-Pop Fan If You Pretend to Like Other Idols to make your partner jealous

It is obvious when you pretend to be a K-pop fangirl and fanboy. Don’t do it. It is disrespectful to other fangirls and fanboys. Don’t use K-pop as a scapegoat.


#4 You Shouldn’t Date A K-Pop Fan If You Are A Two-Timer to Make Your Partner Jealous

It is not a good way of making your partner jealous. This is what people called as cheating. If you do it, your partner might probably call you as a trash (쓰레기 or ㄱ*새끼) or something. BUT anyway, think twice before you do if you have a conscience.
