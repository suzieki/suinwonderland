How did I change my personality in four years??? (feat. MBTI test)

From INFJ-T to ENTJ-T

My MBTI results show this!!!!

In 2018, the MBTI result showed that I am INFJ-T with 88% introverted. Some people say it is the rarest personality type. Actually, I am an extroverted introvert, or some called it a paradox of the INFJ people.

Sometimes, I want to be with friends 24/7, but a few hours later, I have changed my mind. I left the party by making up excuses. Then, I stay alone for two more days to recharge myself. Some might see me as a homebody, but some might not. My personality is kept changing. Anyway, my introverted mind kept me from going out of my comfort zone in 2018.

In 2019, my personality was changed when I left home to study abroad. I become an ENFJ-T with 68% extraverted. In other words, I am an ENFJ-T—an outgoing weirdo—who likes to entertain people by doing funny things in front of many people. Once, I did silly dance with confidence in front of seniors and classmates at the leadership camp. That is not the first time though. I did several times within a year. Trust me. I am not drunk, but I dance like a crazy drunk dancer. And I do not even know where that confidence comes from. After a year, thanks to COVID-19 -_-, my little introverted side is back, but I am still 57% extraverted. The MBTI result showed that I am ENTJ-T. I sometimes show my confidence, but some people misjudged me as arrogant. Anyway, my personality will keep changing, so I just accept myself for who am, but still need to interact with people. Anyway, just keep it in mind “Nothing is permanent and human are social animal”
