Let me introduce them to you.......
Instead of telling you everything about them, I’ll give you their profile. Just take a look at it. Here it is, but I only give it to you because you are one of the readers of this story.



As you can see in the profile that I gave you, he is Yoshi.
[I’ll leave you to imagine his appearance because there is no photo of him in the profile. Sorry about that, but I’ll do my best to describe his personality and physical appearance.]

Anyway, you’ll slowly get to know her if you continue reading this story. Then, shall we read another profile?
