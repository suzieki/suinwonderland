K-POP MV Streaming on YouTube to Increase Views

SU's YouTube Streaming Guide for All K-POP Fandoms

April 24, 2021

Here are some tips on how to stream MV like a human without losing views. NOT like a programmed bot.


1. Do NOT Use VPN

VPN stands for Virtual Private Network and is a method I DO NOT recommend using for streaming. Why don't I recommend using a VPN for streaming?
When surfing the internet, VPN blocks your IP address so the site/app can't detect where you're connecting from. Some even go as far as to change your IP address so it looks like you're accessing the site from a different location.
This would be considered cheating because, if used, the user residing outside of the area they're using a VPN for would be manipulating data.


2. Do Not Log Out of Your YOUTUBE Account!

This is the best way to verify your effort in streaming as a genuine one. Even if you want to use incognito you should still log into your account.


3. Do Not Stream Continuously/On Loop!

This is because it may be counted as spam and it does not help to increase views. It also ended up risking previous views from your account because YOUTUBE deleted all your views in the MV views record.


4. Watch Other Videos In Between

This will also help with verifying your streams. Make sure you don’t have ‘Auto Play’ on so you can manually click on other videos.


5. Use Playlists

If you don’t wanna do it manually, another alternative option is using playlists. It is an easier method especially if you do not have the time to manually click on videos (or are sleeping) as it has the same outcome.


6. Watch Full MV (Don’t skip in between. Watch till the end)

Watch the full video without skipping or increasing the speed. Please note that skipping advertisements does not affect your streaming. However, watching full ads (and clicking on them) will help to increase the ad revenue.


7. Stream on A Maximum of Two Devices

Stream on A MAXIMUM OF TWO DEVICES connected to the same wi-fi source as YouTube also tracks the IP addresses to verify streams. If you have a laptop and a tablet connected to the same wi-fi, you can stream without having issues. However, to take an extra precautionary measure, you should not play the video at the same time on both devices. You can prevent this by having a playlist on shuffle on one of the devices. Moreover, you should log in to a different YouTube account on the second device if you can. If you want to use your phone for streaming as well, you can do so if it is your second device by following the steps above. By using your phone’s mobile data instead of connecting it to the wi-fi, you will be able to stream with it as a 3rd device because it will have a different IP address.


8. Share the Link To MV

If you want to help with the MV trending in your country, you can do so by sharing the link of the video online (anywhere you can and it is relevant - SNS, forums, blogs, media outlets, websites - through posts or comments) and do so multiple times during the first 24 hours. This will not only help to trend the MV, but it will also increase GP (general public) reach and interest and thus it will increase the view count.


9. Embedded MV

Watching the embedded MV on Facebook or websites will help to increase the views on top of streaming on YouTube!
