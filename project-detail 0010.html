EXO member D.O. will be debuting as a soloist

July 19, 2021

The long wait is over! D.O.'s solo debut is coming!

D.O. will release his debut Solo Mini-Album ‘Empathy’ on 26th July at 6 PM KST SM Entertainment released an announcement on 25th June, which stated that D.O.’s first mini-album will be dropped soon. Later, it is confirmed that D.O’s debut solo mini-album titled 공감 (Empathy)—the eight-track project—will be released on 26 July at 6 PM KST and can pre-order the album from 1st July onwards.

As TMI, EXO member D.O., also known as Do Kyung-soo, has been discharged from mandatory military service on Monday, January 25 after enlisting in July 1, 2019. He released a live video of his latest track "That's Okay"—a ballad song released in July 2019—on the evening of his discharge.
