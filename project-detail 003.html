Kingdom: Legendary War

April 1, 2021

Kingdom: Legendary War is the sequel to the reality survival show Road To Kingdom, which was also sequel to Queendom. Kingdom will feature 6 famous male K-Pop groups. The winner of Road To Kingdom, The Boyz, is joining the rest of the groups in Kingdom. The show will begin airing on April 1st, 2021, on Mnet.
