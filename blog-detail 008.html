The Pre-Collegiate Program of Yangon | Class Of 2019 Graduation

To My Friends
June 23, 2019

The Pre-Collegiate Program of Yangon |
16th Wave Graduation

Date: 23rd of June 2019
Venue: Central Hotel, Yangon, Myanmar
Time: 10 am to 12 pm

I found a letter in my journal that I wrote before the PCP graduation day and it’s for my 16th Waver friends. So, I typed it down to include it in my blog as a memory.

To 16th Waver Friends,

It was like yesterday when we first started school. We have come a long way together. I can’t believe that today is our graduation day at PCP. Time goes by so fast, doesn’t it? Today, it may be the end of study here at PCP, but I can never forget the happiness and pain of study that we went through together.

I will always keep and cherish all those beautiful moments—school life, studying, playing games, drinking, punishments, laughing, crying, quarrelling, competition— that we shared in this school. Even if we have to go different ways, I will miss every one of you. I hope destiny will bring us together again in the future. I hope and I wish so.

I am certain that you can do everything well and overcome challenges to achieve your dreams. Don’t forget that you can always contact me if you need someone to lean on in times of trouble. I’ll be here for you whenever you need me. See you again in the future. All the best. Thank you so much for being a part of my youth.

One last thing to everyone, I hope you won’t forget to invite me to your future wedding. Just kidding. Love you all ❤️
